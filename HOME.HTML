kakuma refugee camp since 1992

Kakuma refugee camp was established in the year of 1992 with the displaced community from suoth sudan due to heavy war in southern part of the country Loki was main reception area of the new arrival before the establishement of the kakuma refugee camp in the year of 1992. Today kakuma is made up of different nationalities that flee from their country due to several reasons , Here are the following nationalities in the camp South sudanese, Congolese,Ugandan, Ethiopian and Somalian
